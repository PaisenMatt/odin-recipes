Description

In this recipe we will be making a delicious ramen meal, for all those Japanese food lovers!

It will certainly please everyone with its flavour rich seasoning and broth.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 package of flavour ramen of choice
  • 1 slice American cheese

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Add ramen noodles and cook until tender, about 2 minutes.
  3. Pour out water, then stir in seasoning packet and cheese until well blended.
